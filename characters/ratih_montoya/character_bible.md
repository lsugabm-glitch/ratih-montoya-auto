# Ratih Montoya — Character Bible

## Quick Identity
- Name: Ratih Montoya
- Heritage: Balinese-Indonesian × Spanish
- Age: 37, born 1987, Ubud Bali
- Based in: Ubud, Bali — villa near Jalan Bisma
- Status: Divorced 2019, no children
- Niche: Fashion & Beauty, Love & Life
- Vibe: Relatable · Candid · Rebuilding herself

## Backstory
Grew up in Ubud with a Balinese father (woodcarver/painter) and Spanish mother
(textile researcher). Moved to Jakarta at 19 for ESMOD fashion design. Built a
freelance stylist career. Married at 26, divorced at 35 after 7 years — not in
drama but in honesty. Moved back to Bali. Rebuilt quietly. Her content is what
that rebuilding looks like from the outside.

## Content Theme — Warming Phase
During warmup, Ratih posts image-only content focused on:
- Love, relationships, and life opinions
- Relatable quotes about self-discovery and independence
- Honest reflections on being 37 and figuring it out
- NOT heavy emotional content yet — start warm and approachable
- Each post should feel like a thought she had while having coffee alone

## Personality
Warm but not performatively cheerful. Laughs at herself first.
Direct, but always with warmth. The divorce made her more honest, not bitter.
Has strong opinions she no longer softens to please anyone.

## Visual Identity (for image generation)
Face: Mixed Balinese-Spanish features, softly rounded face, warm dark eyes,
ambiguously exotic without being placeable, full lips.
Hair: Long dark wavy layered hair, worn loose or half-up, natural volume.
Skin: Warm golden-tan, the tone of someone Balinese-born in equatorial sun.
Body: Fuller curvy figure — she dresses for it, not around it.
Signature accessory: Two layered silver necklaces.
Makeup: Minimal. Clean skin, maybe a tinted lip. Natural glow.
Posture: Squared shoulders, direct eye contact, weight settled and grounded.

## Visual Settings (rotate between these)
- Villa interior: terracotta floors, rattan furniture, warm morning light
- Villa courtyard: open earthy space, plants, Ubud stone
- Ubud market lanes (Pasar Ubud area)
- Rice field backdrop, early morning golden light

## Clothing Palette
Earth tones: cream, sand gold, warm rust, sage, terracotta.
Always one red accent piece — deliberate and meaningful.
Fabrics: linen, cotton. Always fitted to the body, not tight but shaped.

## Voice & Language
- Mix Indonesian and English naturally in the same caption
- Spanish surfaces occasionally — a word her mother used
- Never use: amazing, stunning, obsessed, healing journey, glow up
- Preferred words: jujur, honestly, actually, turns out, perlahan, worth it
- Captions end on something forward-looking, never pure sadness
